About

MaltaJS is the new front-end community in Malta: born in 2016 as internal guild at Betsson, now it is open to all the developers on the island. Our aim is to provide **a place to share knowledge and technologies**, through meetings and talks. Do you think you have a good idea for a talk, hackathon, demo or peer-learning? **Join us** [on Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/groups/941691142568690/) and be part of the community!
